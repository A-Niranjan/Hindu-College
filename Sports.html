Sports in our campus

There are many competition in our college and encourging students for various inter college competition

Cricket

Our college cricket team fosters a spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship, honing batting, bowling, and fielding skills through rigorous practice and competitive matches.

Football

The college football team ignites passion and determination on the field, strategizing formations and showcasing agility, speed, and control in the pursuit of victory.

Volleyball

The college volleyball team combines athleticism and strategic thinking, with players perfecting their setting, spiking, and serving techniques to dominate the court.

Throwball

The college throwball team cultivates an energetic and fast-paced environment, emphasizing accuracy, teamwork, and agility as players strive to outscore their opponents.

Kabbadi

The college kabaddi team embodies the values of strength, strategy, and resilience, as players engage in exhilarating encounters that test their physical prowess and tactical ingenuity.

Athletics

The college athletics program provides a platform for individuals to excel in various track and field events, encompassing sprinting, jumping, throwing, and long-distance running, fostering dedication and a drive for personal bests.

Indoor Games

Puzzles: Jigsaw puzzles, crosswords, sudoku, or any brain teaser that challenges you.
Board games: Some solo board games offer engaging gameplay, like Robinson Crusoe or Friday.
Card games: Solitaire or Patience are classic single-player card games.
